Internal linkLinks are found in nearly all web pages. Links allow users to click their way from page to page. HTML Links - Hyperlinks You can click on a link and jump to another document. When you move the mouse over a link, the mouse arrow will turn into a little hand. Note: A link does not have to be text. It can be an image or any other HTML element.
